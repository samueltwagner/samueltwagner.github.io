About Me

Hello there! I was raised in Charleston, South Carolina. I've had an affinity for computers, video games, and technology ever since I was a kid. In High School, I took an engineering class that fascinated me. I realized that everything from electricy to robots to programming revolves around 1's and 0's! That connection really blew my mind.

I'm now furthing my understanding by majoring in Computer Information Technology with a particular interest in Software Development.

Over the summer of 2017, I was a full-time intern using C#, bootstrap, jQuery, SQL, and the Asp.net MVC framework to build an eforms (electronicForms) platform that helped the company save paper, money, and time.
